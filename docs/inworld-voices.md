## Notes

- **Price:** TTS-1.5 Max = $10/1M chars | TTS-1.5 Mini = $5/1M chars
- **Speed control:** 0.5× to 1.5× native rate
- **Temperature:** 0–2 (default 1.0; lower = more stable, higher = more expressive)
- **Voice cloning:** instant (15s audio) or professional (30+ min audio)
- **Current project default:** uses OpenAI `onyx` — consider switching to Inworld for longer content
